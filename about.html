Starry Public Library is a small single branch library located in South Palo, CA. It was founded in 1977.

It's maintained by a staff of 5; the Library Director, 2 librarians, and 2 library assistants. The library is heavily influenced by the Star Wars world as developed by George Lucas.

Library Director - Katie Stinson

Prior to joining Starry Public Library, Katie was a Senior Customer Success Manager for Boopsie, and then Demco Software, after their acquisition. During her nearly five-year career with the company, she represented over 400 library customers with a 96% retention rate and assisted with a 7% growth to her sales team’s territory. In 2016, she was awarded the Mariano Rivera Lazarus Award for the most saves and revivals of customer accounts.

The University of Michigan was where Katie’s love of libraries turned into a career prospect. While working the night shift at the Harlan Hatcher Graduate Library, she realized that working for a library could be a full-time career, and that there was even a degree that you could earn too! She learned about that too late in her time at Michigan and went the route of getting a degree in Latin American and Caribbean Studies (still a very valuable tool). Several years later, she’s renewing her passion for libraries in the academic realm and is now taking classes at San Jose State University's iSchool.

Katie believes that education and libraries are vital to the success of a community, which is why she finds peace working with libraries and library-adjacent companies.

Katie is a Michigan-native, now in California, and still makes several trips to the mountains for a snow fix. She has two lovable dogs and a mischievous cat, all alumni of the San Francisco SPCA. An avid reader, you’ll often find her with her iPhone/iPad/computer in hand with her latest eBook find.
